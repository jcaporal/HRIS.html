2. Projected Annual Savings

The savings are based on a conservative estimate of reduced manual workload across HRIS, Payroll, and Business Intelligence (BI) teams. To achieve a more precise calculation, a time and motion study would be required to quantify the specific hours saved for each task. These hours would then be monetized using the average salary of the team members involved, providing a more accurate and defendable figure for annual savings. This granular approach would lead to a more compelling business case for the investment.

Granular Breakdown of Projected Savings
  • Baseline Savings (HRIS + Payroll + BI Teams):
    • Manual workload reduction: 20 hours/month
    • Estimated value: $5/hour
    • Calculation: (20 hours/month) x (12 months) x ($5/hour) = $1,200
  • Indirect Savings (Adjusted Upward to ~$15,000):
    • Avoided time-to-payroll corrections from the Payroll Team: Estimated at $5,000
    • Avoided data remediation from the BI, Data Science, and Workforce Management (WFM) teams: Estimated at $7,000
    • Reduced errors and compliance risks (e.g., penalty avoidance, productivity gains): Estimated at $1,800

Note: The savings figures and financial model are based on a series of conservative assumptions for illustrative purposes. A formal study would be required to validate these numbers. Additionally, the financial model simplifies costs by treating them as fixed across all vendors. However, since BambooHR and Workday typically charge on a per-employee basis, their costs would rise as headcount grows. Factoring this in would further increase their annual costs, deepen the negative NPV, and extend the ROI timeline.
